**Mission:** Enable institutional-grade private trading on blockchain through zero-knowledge proofs, making DeFi accessible to sophisticated traders and institutions who require confidentiality.

**Goal:** Become the leading privacy-preserving trading infrastructure on Ethereum, processing billions in private trade volume while maintaining perfect privacy guarantees.
